Henan University History Timeline

Witness the century-old glory of Henan University

1912

Founding of the University

Founded as Henan Preparatory School for Studying in Europe and America, laying the foundation for higher education in Henan Province.

1939

Anti-Japanese War Period

Moved to Xixia County during the Anti-Japanese War, adhering to the mission of running a school and cultivating talents despite hardships.

1942

Promoted to National University

Officially promoted to National Henan University, becoming one of the key national universities at that time.

1984

Renamed Officially

Renamed Henan University officially, accelerating the pace of discipline construction and talent training.

2017

Double First-Class Initiative

Selected into China's "Double First-Class" Initiative, entering a new stage of high-quality development.

2022

110th Anniversary

Celebrated the 110th anniversary of its founding, inheriting the school spirit and forging ahead into the future.
