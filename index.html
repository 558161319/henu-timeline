Henan University History Timeline

Witness the century-old glory of Henan University

1912

Founding: Preparatory School for Overseas Study

Founded in 1912 on the site of the former Qing Dynasty Imperial Examination Academy, the Preparatory School for Overseas Study in Europe and America marked the birth of modern higher education in Henan. It was one of China’s three major overseas study preparatory schools, alongside the predecessors of Tsinghua University and Shanghai Jiao Tong University, nurturing a new generation of students to pursue advanced studies abroad and bring modern knowledge back to China.

1923

Official Establishment: Zhongzhou University

In 1923, the school was officially renamed Zhongzhou University, transitioning from a preparatory school to a full comprehensive university with liberal arts and science departments. This milestone solidified its status as a leading institution in central China, with distinguished scholars such as Feng Youlan serving as department heads, laying the foundation for its century-long academic legacy.

1939 - 1944

Relocation & Wartime Education at Tantou

During the War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression, the university relocated hundreds of miles west to Tantou, a remote mountain area, to continue teaching under harsh conditions. In 1942, it was upgraded to National Henan University, becoming one of the few national universities in China at the time. The perseverance of faculty and students embodied the "unyielding and self-reliant" spirit that remains the core of Henan University’s identity.

1952

National Departmental Adjustment

The nationwide restructuring of higher education in 1952 reshaped Henan University significantly. Its strong programs in agriculture, medicine, and engineering were separated to form or support new institutions, including Henan Agricultural University, Henan Medical University (now part of Zhengzhou University), and Wuhan University. While this reduced the university’s scale, it contributed to the development of higher education across China.

1984

Restoration of the Name "Henan University"

After decades of changes (including renamings as Henan Teachers College and Henan Normal University), the school officially restored its historic name Henan University in 1984. This marked a new era of development, reaffirming its century-old identity and opening a path for growth in the reform era.

2008

Became a key co-constructed university

In 2008, Henan University became a key university co-constructed by Henan Province and the Ministry of Education. This landmark partnership provided enhanced support for its academic development, infrastructure, and research capacity, laying critical groundwork for its future rise as a leading institution in central China.

2017

"Double First-Class" Initiative

In 2017, Henan University was selected as a "Double First-Class" construction university, with Biology chosen as a world-class discipline. This milestone recognized the university’s decades of progress in life sciences, particularly its research in cotton biology and molecular genetics, and accelerated its drive toward building a globally competitive academic profile.

2021

Launch of Zhengzhou Campus

The new Zhengzhou campus opened in September 2021, located in Zhengdong New Area’s Higher Education Park. Designed with traditional Chinese architectural aesthetics and modern facilities, the campus represents a new chapter for the century-old university, expanding its educational footprint and creating a hub for interdisciplinary research and innovation in the provincial capital.

2022

110th Anniversary Celebration

In 2022, Henan University marked its 110th anniversary, reflecting on its journey from a small preparatory school to a comprehensive "Double First-Class" university. The celebration brought together alumni, faculty, and students to honor the university’s legacy of resilience and to outline its vision for the next century as a leading institution in central China.

2025

110th Anniversary

As part of the mid-term evaluation of its "Double First-Class" construction, Henan University has achieved significant breakthroughs in scientific research, academic output, and disciplinary development. Key progress includes growth in high-impact publications, national research grants, and international collaborations, driving its continued rise as a top-tier university.
